Search engine optimisiation

Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) means making your website more attractive to search engines such as Google and Bing. It involves ‘going behind the scenes’ of your site, identifying words and phrases which accurately describe your business and inserting these in the invisible parts of the site (known as meta data) which Google and other search engines look at.
